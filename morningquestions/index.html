About Morning Questions

Morning Questions is your daily companion for emotional wellness and productivity. Start each day with purpose through guided reflection, emotion tracking, and task management.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my daily reflection?

Open the app and begin your morning reflection. Follow the guided prompts to record your thoughts, emotions, and tasks for the day.

How do I track my emotions?

In the emotion tracking section, you can select your current mood and add optional notes to provide context for your emotional state.

Can I edit my tasks later?

Yes, you can edit, update, or mark tasks as complete at any time by accessing the task management section.

Is my data private?

Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal information on external servers.
